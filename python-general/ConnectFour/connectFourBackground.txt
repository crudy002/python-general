The game to create is Connect Four

Connect fouri s a game in which 2 plays (red, yellow) compete on a 6x6 grid, by 
taking turns dropping circular tokens into the slots.

Objective: Make four tokens of the your color in a row in one of the four winning orientations
    1) Vertical
    2) Horizontal
    3) Diagonal
    4) Anti-diagonal
